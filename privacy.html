Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 18, 2026

1. Who We Are

MQ Steel Corp is a structural steel fabrication and erection company. This privacy policy explains how we handle information submitted through our website at mq-steel-corp.web.app (and any associated custom domain).

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: our contact form.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • Name — to address your inquiry personally
  • Email address — to respond to your request
  • Company name (optional) — to understand your organizational context
  • Project description — to evaluate the scope of your request

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or behavioral analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to respond to your project inquiry. Specifically:

  • To evaluate and reply to your request for steel fabrication or erection services
  • To follow up on ongoing project discussions you have initiated

We will never use your contact information for unsolicited marketing, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of MQ Steel Corp and its direct operational partners without your explicit consent.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Form submissions are stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure in the United States. All data is:

  • Transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit)
  • Stored with access restricted to authorized MQ Steel Corp personnel only
  • Protected by Firestore security rules that prevent any public read or modification

Google's data center security practices apply to the underlying infrastructure. See Google's Cloud Security overview for details.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain reasonable business records. If you would like your submission deleted, contact us and we will remove it within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Service Purpose Data involved
Google Firebase Website hosting and form data storage Form submission data, server request logs
Google reCAPTCHA v3 Bot and spam protection on the contact form Browser/device signals for fraud detection (no form data)

The Google Privacy Policy and Terms of Service apply to all Google services listed above.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or user tracking. The reCAPTCHA service may set a session cookie as part of its bot-detection process. This cookie does not track your browsing activity across other websites.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have legal rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or restrict processing

To exercise any of these rights, use our contact form. We will respond within 30 days. If you are located in the European Union, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business inquiries and is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
