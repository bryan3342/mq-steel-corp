  from google.com / carried a third-party watermark (web-sourced reference
  images, not MQ Steel's own work). They were left out so the site only shows
  genuine MQ Steel work.

  not identifiable). Since these are MQ Steel's own workers, that's normally
  fine — but confirm you're comfortable displaying them publicly.
- **Readable third-party signage** was avoided where possible; a couple of
  shots contain minor background text.
