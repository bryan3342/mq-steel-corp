Building America's infrastructure with precision and integrity

MQ Steel Corp is a leading structural steel fabrication and erection company dedicated to delivering exceptional quality on every project. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, precision, and a commitment to safety that is second to none.

From commercial buildings and industrial warehouses to complex architectural structures, our team brings expertise and craftsmanship to every beam and bolt. We work closely with general contractors, architects, and engineers to ensure seamless project execution from design to completion.

In partnership with IronWorks, we combine our hands-on construction expertise with advanced fabrication technology to deliver projects on time and within budget, no matter the scale or complexity.

Meet the Owner

The leadership behind MQ Steel Corp

MQ Steel Corp Leadership

Founder & CEO

With a deep-rooted passion for structural steel and over 15 years of hands-on experience in the construction industry, the founder of MQ Steel Corp has built the company from the ground up. Starting as a welder and ironworker, their journey through every facet of steel construction provides a unique perspective that prioritizes quality craftsmanship and worker safety above all.

Under their leadership, MQ Steel Corp has grown from a small local operation to a trusted partner for general contractors across the region. Their philosophy is simple: every project deserves the same level of attention, precision, and dedication, whether it is a small storefront or a multi-story commercial building.
